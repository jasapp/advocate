# AI Customer Service Advocate

## The Mission: Digital Warfare Against Predatory AI Systems

This is not a typical software project. This is a weapon system designed to fight back against corporations using AI to harm people.

## Why This Exists - The Brutal Reality

**Real-world scenarios this system is designed to combat:**

### Insurance Company AI Auto-Denials
- Stage 4 cancer patient needs treatment
- Insurance company's AI auto-denies the claim based on "experimental treatment" classification
- Patient must navigate a maze of appeals, phone trees, and deliberately adversarial AI chatbots
- Each delay could be fatal
- The system is DESIGNED to exhaust the patient until they give up or die
- **Our system fights this battle on their behalf**

### Dark Pattern Cancellation Flows
- User tries to cancel a subscription they no longer need
- Company hides cancellation behind 4+ layers of navigation
- Fear-based messaging ("your safety features will stop working")
- Multiple retention popups with psychological manipulation
- Mandatory surveys designed as friction
- Technical defenses (overlays, timing tricks) to block automation
- **This is theft by design - our system automates the fight**

### The Power Imbalance
- Corporations have unlimited resources, armies of UX designers, and AI systems optimized to extract maximum profit
- Individual users are exhausted, confused, and give up
- The system counts on human fatigue
- **We give users an AI that never gets tired, never gives up, and learns from every battle**

## Technical Approach

This system uses:
- **Web automation** (Playwright/Selenium) to navigate adversarial UIs
- **AI APIs** (Claude, GPT, Grok) for natural language interaction
- **Genetic algorithms** to evolve effective strategies against new defenses
- **JavaScript injection** to bypass clickjacking and other technical barriers
- **Systematic documentation** of dark patterns for future battles

## What This System Does

1. **Accepts user requests**: "Cancel my subscription" or "Appeal my insurance denial"
2. **Selects strategies**: Based on company, issue type, and historical success rates
3. **Executes conversations**: Uses AI to interact with corporate chatbots/systems
4. **Bypasses defenses**: Handles dark patterns, technical barriers, retention tactics
5. **Never gives up**: Continues until success or genuine human intervention required
6. **Learns and adapts**: Genetic algorithms evolve better strategies over time
